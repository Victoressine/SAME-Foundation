How Local Leadership Transforms Schools

Local leadership plays a vital role in transforming schools and shaping the future of education. When communities take ownership of their educational systems, the results are often more impactful and sustainable than those imposed externally. Principals, teachers, parents, and community leaders who understand the unique challenges can create practical, relevant solutions to improve attendance, learning outcomes, and community engagement.

SAME Foundation 2025 Educational Impact Report
In Collaboration with RixonOC Management Consulting and Communications, LLC (USA)

Date: 1st October 2025
Prepared by: Senior Educational Consultant, Shakers and Movers Educational (SAME) Foundation

Introduction:
The Shakers and Movers Educational (SAME) Foundation, in partnership with RixonOC Management Consulting and Communications, LLC in the United States, has made remarkable strides in advancing educational access and opportunity for Ghanaian students in 2025. Program Overview:
In the first half of 2025, the SAME Foundation successfully advised and supported three Ghanaian master‚Äôs students, and one undergraduate helping them secure scholarships totaling USD 35,000 (approximately GHC 455,000) to pursue graduate studies in Turkey. This initiative represents more than just financial assistance; it embodies the Foundation‚Äôs mission to bridge educational gaps and foster global competence among Africa‚Äôs emerging leaders.

Program Objectives:

  • To promote access to higher education for deserving Ghanaian students through international partnerships.
  • To develop globally minded scholars capable of contributing to sustainable development in Ghana and across Africa.
  • To create a generational ripple effect by empowering students who will, in turn, inspire others within their communities.

Key Achievements:

  • 4 master‚Äôs students successfully enrolled in reputable Turkish universities for postgraduate studies in diverse fields, including economics, education, public administration, and communication.
  • USD 35,000 (GHC 455,000) in scholarship awards secured within six months, easing the financial burden on students and their families.
  • Establishment of an academic advising model that connects Ghanaian students with international opportunities through credible partnerships.
  • Strengthened collaboration between SAME Foundation and RixonOC Management Consulting and Communications, LLC, laying the groundwork for a long-term international education pipeline.

By enabling access to quality undergraduate and graduate education abroad, the SAME Foundation is nurturing a new generation of Ghanaian leaders equipped with the global knowledge, critical thinking, and professional skills necessary to drive innovation and sustainable change upon their return.

Community Empowerment: These scholars serve as role models and catalysts for other young Ghanaians, demonstrating that international education is attainable through hard work, mentorship, and strategic support.

Knowledge Transfer: Graduates are expected to contribute to local development through research, leadership, and community-based projects upon completion of their programs.

Generational Transformation: As these students progress in their careers, they will uplift families and communities, fostering a cycle of educational advancement and socio-economic mobility.

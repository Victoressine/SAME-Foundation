Leadership Team

Dr. Rixon O. Campbell

President & Chief Executive Officer, SAME Foundation

Dr. Rixon O. Campbell is the President and Chief Executive Officer of the SAME Foundation, a global nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing education, community development, and youth empowerment across Africa and the diaspora. A visionary leader and strategist, Dr. Campbell has built a distinguished career focused on transforming lives through access, opportunity, and leadership development.

Under his leadership, the SAME Foundation has established impactful partnerships that have provided scholarships, skills training, and community infrastructure improvements in underserved regions, particularly in Ghana. His work reflects a lifelong commitment to creating generational change through education, sustainable development, and global collaboration.

Dr. Campbell is also an accomplished organizational development expert, educator, and international consultant with extensive experience in higher education, multicultural affairs, and nonprofit leadership. His belief in the power of service, inclusion, and global citizenship continues to inspire the Foundation’s mission to empower communities and shape future leaders.

Mr. Al-Hassan Kodwo Baidoo

Chairman, SAME Foundation Ghana

Mr. Al-Hassan Kodwo Baidoo is an educationist and development advocate with extensive experience in educational leadership, youth empowerment, and community development. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Industrial Arts from KNUST, a Master’s degree in Educational Leadership and Innovation from the University of Education, Winneba, and a Master of Philosophy in Educational Leadership and School Improvement from Methodist University, Ghana.

As Chairman of SAME Foundation Ghana, he provides strategic leadership in advancing the Foundation’s mission to promote quality education, child welfare, and women’s empowerment. Mr. Baidoo also leads the Green Saints Foundation, a school-based climate change initiative, and serves as an Executive Member of Junior Shapers Africa.

He brings to his work a wealth of experience from his earlier roles in corporate management and a lifelong commitment to education as a driver of social transformation.

Mr. Robert Emmanuel Kaye

Co-Chairman, SAME Foundation Ghana

Robert Emmanuel Kaye is a seasoned Client Service and Research Professional with over a decade of experience in market research, client management, and strategic insight across Africa. He has worked with top organizations such as IPSOS Ghana, Kantar Millward Brown, and Bureau of Market and Social Research, delivering impactful solutions for leading brands including MTN, Coca-Cola, Nestlé, Vodafone, and Guinness Ghana.

He holds a BSc from the University of Ghana and professional certifications from the University of Georgia, the Graduate School of Governance and Leadership, and the Banking College of Ghana.

Robert is also a passionate advocate for health and safety and actively supports community initiatives that promote education and youth empowerment.

Dr. Thelma Frimpong Mensah

Women & Girl Empowerment, SAME Foundation Ghana

Dr. Thelma Frimpong Mensah is an accomplished leader with expertise in ESG and sustainability, business administration, and finance. She holds a Doctorate in Business Administration from SMC University in Switzerland, an MBA in Finance from Sikkim Manipal University in India, and a BA in Sociology and Political Science from the University of Ghana.

She currently serves as Billing Operations Manager and CFO at Olidia Care Inc. (USA), Governing Council Member of the Institute of Project Management Professionals (Ghana), and Senior Vice President of Education at the African Youth Institute.

With previous experience as Senior Consultant at Deloitte Ghana and CEO of Desert Dew Services and Consultancy, Dr. Mensah is also a respected speaker passionate about women empowerment, entrepreneurship, and education.
